Ingredients

  • 4-5 fresh lemons
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 6 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Squeeze the juice from the lemons into a pitcher.
  2. Dissolve sugar in 1 cup of hot water to create a simple syrup.
  3. Pour the simple syrup into the pitcher with lemon juice.
  4. Add cold water and stir well.
  5. Chill in the fridge for at least an hour.
  6. Serve over ice and garnish with fresh mint leaves.
