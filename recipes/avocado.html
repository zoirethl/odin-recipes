Awesome Avocado Toast 🥑🍞

Upgrade your breakfast game with the Awesome Avocado Toast! Creamy mashed avocado on perfectly toasted bread—a simple yet heavenly combination. Seasoned with a pinch of salt, pepper, and optional red pepper flakes for that extra kick. A splash of lemon juice ties it all together, making each bite a burst of flavor. Ready to elevate your mornings? Say hello to your new favorite toast! 🌅

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of your favorite bread
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • A splash of lemon juice

Steps

  1. Toast the bread to your liking.
  2. Mash the ripe avocado and spread it evenly on the toasted slices.
  3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes for a kick.
  4. Finish with a splash of lemon juice.
  5. Enjoy your Instagram-worthy avocado toast! 📸
